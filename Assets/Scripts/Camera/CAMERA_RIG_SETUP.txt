Camera 架子的推荐配法（独立 CameraPosition，不挂在 Player 下）

层级:
  CameraPosition
  ├── CameraFollowTarget (Target=玩家, World Offset / Smooth Time)
  ├── PlayerCameraOrbitPivot (同物体; Q/E 转台, XY 地面选 Z Up)
  └── Main Camera
        SimpleFollowCamera:
          Follow Target = 玩家
          Update World Position 必须关闭（子相机）
          Focus：Follow Point Offset / Look At Offset
          Position：可选 Apply Offset As Local Position + Offset From Focus
          Rotation：Aim At Focus（Pivot 下只调俯仰）；镜头平滑 = 瞄准时旋转平滑时间 Lens Smooth Time
        关掉 CinemachineBrain

不用 Cinemachine 时请禁用场景里的 CinemachineCamera 与 Brain.
